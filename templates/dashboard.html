How It Works

Algorithm 01
🔄 Cycle Detection
Detects circular fund routing (A→B→C→A) using Tarjan's Strongly Connected Components algorithm. An SCC with ≥3 nodes means every member can reach every other — indicating a money loop. Suspicion score contribution: +35
O(V + E)
Algorithm 02
🐙 Smurfing Detection
Identifies fan-in (≥5 senders → 1 receiver) and fan-out (1 sender → ≥5 receivers) within a 72-hour sliding window. Uses vectorized NumPy timestamp arithmetic for performance. Score contribution: +25
O(T log T)
Algorithm 03
🧅 Layered Shell Networks
BFS from high-activity accounts through shell intermediaries (≤3 total transactions). Chains of 3+ hops through shells simulate how funds are obfuscated before reaching the final destination. Score: +20
O(V + E)
Algorithm 04
🎯 Hub Node Analysis
Accounts at or above the 95th percentile of combined in+out degree are flagged as laundering hubs. Legitimate high-volume merchants are automatically whitelisted (degree ≥200, diversity ratio ≥60%). Score: +20
O(V)
